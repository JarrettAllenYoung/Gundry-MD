To update your address in your account:

Log in to My Health Account by clicking here.

Access Profile Settings: Once logged in, use the Top Right Menu dropdown to go to Profile & Settings.

Update your Address: In Profile & Settings, you’ll find options to update your Billing Address and Shipping Address.

Save Changes: When you’ve completed all updates, click the Save Account Info button at the bottom of the page to ensure your changes are saved.

If you’re still having trouble, contact us for assistance from one of our specialized agents. (800) 852-0477
